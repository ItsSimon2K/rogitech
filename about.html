About us

Rogitech Technologies

Rogitech was founded by Bram Bakker and Luuk Jansen in Amsterdam, Netherlands. It is one of the top gaming accesories manufacturer since 2016. With recent market expansion, we now have over 500 stores worldwide and ships to all countries around the world.

We specialize in mice, keyboards, headsets and speakers to produce the best quality of its kind. With more than 50 hours put into each product, only the best make the cut.
