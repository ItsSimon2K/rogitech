About us

Rogitech Technologies

Rogitech was founded by Bram Bakker and Luuk Jansen in Amsterdam, Netherlands. It is one of the top gaming accessories manufacturers since 2016. With its recent market expansion, it now has over 500 stores worldwide and ships to all countries around the world.

Our team specializes in mice, keyboards, headsets, and speakers to produce the best quality of its kind. With countless hours put into each product, only the best make the cut.
